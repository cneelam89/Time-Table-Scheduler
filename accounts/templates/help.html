How it works:

1. Register yourself to start generating the timetable
2. Log into the System using your account
3. Input the Information for the Courses
4. Input the Information for the Professors
5. Input the Information for the Rooms
6. Input the Information for the Class Timings
7. Input the Information for the Departments
8. Input the Information for the Sections
9. Click on Home and then Click on "Generate Timetable"
10. The generated timetable will be accessible for viewing.
